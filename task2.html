Macan

Macan is a Russian singer and songwriter who became famous thanks to his emotional and melodic songs. His style mixes pop and rap, and his lyrics talk about love, feelings, and everyday stories. In a short time, he gained a huge audience and became one of the most popular young artists in the country.

Key stages

  1. 2020 — first tracks released online
  2. 2021 — songs reached millions of streams
  3. 2022 — performed at concerts and festivals
  4. 2023 — released full albums and gained wide recognition

Main features of his music

  • Emotional and lyrical lyrics
  • Mix of rap verses and melodic choruses
  • Close interaction with fans on social media
